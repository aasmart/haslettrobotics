About Us: Promoting STEM

The Haslett Robotics Club is a community organization founded in 2008 with the intent of promoting STEM by instructing K-12 aged students in the fundamentals of engineering and programming using basic robotics systems as well as encouraging fair play and sportsmanship for all youth residing in the Haslett, Okemos, and adjoining areas.
