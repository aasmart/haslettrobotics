About Us

Promoting STEM

The Haslett Robotics Club is a community organization founded in 2008 to provide instruction in the fundamentals of engineering and programming using basic robotics systems and encourage fair play and sportsmanship for all youth residing in the Haslett, Okemos, and the adjoining areas.
